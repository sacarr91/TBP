Practicing

Dr. Lisa Maynard
"Lisa Maynard wrote a lot in the early 2000s about practicing and modeling practicing for our students. I feel like this work isn't looked at or thought of enough!" - Dr. Murphy Role of Repetition: This study explores to what extent the role of repetition has in practice activities across four levels of music proficiency. Modeling How to Practice: Help students make the connections at group rehearsals for effective individual practicing
The Role of Repetition Article Modeling how to Practice Article
Maynard, L. (2006). The Role of Repetition in the Practice Sessions of Artist Teachers and Their Students. <i>Bulletin of the Council for Research in Music Education,</i> (167), 61-72. Retrieved August 25, 2021, from http://www.jstor.org/stable/40319290 Maynard, Lisa M. (2005). Modeling how we want students to practice. (EJ726866). ERIC. https://eric.ed.gov/?id=EJ726866

Demographics

Smith, Mick, and alexander
"These are interesting because they describe the general demographic of string orchestra students, teachers, classrooms (all published in the last few years)" - Dr. Murphy The Status of Strings and Orchestra: This study monitors the demographics of a given area, compares these results to previous studies, and describes the findings of these results
The Status of strings and orchestra Article
Smith, B. P., Mick, J. P., & Alexander, M. L. (2018). The Status of Strings and Orchestra Programs in U.S. Schools. String Research Journal, 8(1), 1531. https://doi.org/10.1177/1948499218769607
Elpus and Abril
"These next 2 have high school band and chorus as well but it's interesting to see the difference between band, chorus, and orchestra enrollment." - Dr. Murphy High School Music Ensembles: A study in the national demographics of high school band, choir, and orchestra students and the findings of these results Who Enrolls in High School Music: This study shows a complete demographic of students in high school music ensembles and describes results such as racial/ethnic composition, socioeconomic status, and participation in arts activities outside of school.
